Yes. The information policy is a living document and may be updated to include new requirements, clarifications, or additional details as needed. Any changes will be reviewed and communicated to ensure consistency and transparency.

How does billing work?

Billing is processed on a recurring basis according to the plan or services selected. Invoices are issued before each billing cycle, and payment methods on file are automatically charged unless otherwise arranged.

How do I change my account email?

You can update your account email in the account settings section. Go to Profile or Account Settings, select Edit Email, and enter your new address. A confirmation link will be sent to the new email to verify the change. Once confirmed, your account will be updated.
